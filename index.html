About Me

Hello! I'm Felix Okoth — a passionate software developer, creative designer, and tech entrepreneur based in Kenya. I specialize in building powerful websites, intuitive mobile apps, and digital platforms that help brands stand out and succeed.

With a strong background in both programming and visual design, I focus on crafting digital experiences that are not only functional but also beautiful, fast, and user-friendly. Whether it’s a portfolio, a business website, or a fully-featured app, I deliver quality with detail.

I believe in first impressions and long-term impact. That’s why I ensure every project I touch reflects professionalism, purpose, and personality. Let’s work together and bring your idea to life with creativity and precision.
